About

Actor Lookup is a tool designed for searching specific characters and discovering their respective actor.

1. Search for a Title Name (i.e. "Breaking Bad", "Dexter")
2. Search for a Character Name (i.e. "Walter White", "James Doakes")
3. Select the character to discover information on the actor.

Actor Lookup is powered by imdb-api, every title in the Imdb index is available to search.
Unfortunately some titles may contain no character information, this is due to no cast information being available for that specific title.
